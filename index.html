LOCATION

The Prime Tapsilogan is located in Baluyut Street, Mabuko Sta Cruz, Magalang Pampanga, Philippines of the Magalang Town Center in Sta Cruz, Magalang, Pampanga. With so many people passing by, it's a great area to start a business. The business-owners choose this area because of the crowds, which included numerous tourists, motorcyclists, students, and others.

The entrepreneurs chose our location also because of the cleanliness of the area and the beautiful scenery. Our offers in Prime Tapsilogan are very ideal for individuals who are always in a hurry and behind their schedule. The the owners expect that their guests will most likely be people passing by (passerby), workers, few families, and students as well.
