About Us

  Our very own "Prime Tapsilogan" offers a wide variety of food to select from, starting off with our famous Tapsilog that will give you a juicy and flavorful partnered up with our all-time favorite Houseblend ice tea. Prime tapsilogan will definitely make you feel as if you're eating in the comfort of your homes and will be the exemplary go-to place to dine!

  In view of the fact that the entrepreneurs are inviting all of you to dine and enjoy their meals with ease, they made the tapsilogan's location within reach! Prime Tapsilogan is located in Baluyut Street, Mabuko Sta Cruz,Magalang Pampanga - where a lot of citizens pass by to grab food and beverages or to simply appreciate the beauty of the place. We visualize this as a favorable location in able for people to know Prime Tapsilogan and to encourage them to dine and chill!
